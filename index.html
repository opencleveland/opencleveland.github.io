Want To Help Cleveland?
Good, we could use your help.

We are a volunteer group that contribute our talents by working with our community to improve local government and through relationships and civic technology projects.

We do a lot of things together, but our main activity is our Meeting / Hack Night, usually held on the first Wednesday every month. This meeting is for those who want to:

  • presentations from community members sharing a tech skill or how local government works.
  • start a new project, or find one to join (all backgrounds welcome!)
  • bounce ideas and get help from tech and gov experts
  • learn about open data, civic technology (technology for the public good) and open government
